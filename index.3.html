Gessing Game Code Pen

Project Description

A straight forward game in wich one tries to guess a four digit number. The number itself doesn't have any repeating numbers. You enter a single digit between 0 and 9 in every slot. If the background of the slot turns red, the digit doesn't appear in the number. If the background of the slot turns yellow, that digit appears somewhere in the number but not in that slot. If the slot background turns green you have guessed the right digit in the correct slot. You have ten tries to find the complete number.

Adventure #2: Escaping The Forest Code Pen

Project Description

Adventure #2: Escaping The Forest is the sequel to Adventure #1: Waking Up. Like its predecessor, Adventure #2 is a choose your own adventure game in which you only have two options. One lets you live and the other leads you to an early grave. In this scenario, you are trying to make it out of a forest. As you make your way through the game you will learn why you woke up in Craven Health And Research Center and what secrets were hiding behind its walls. You might also learn what your secret was and why you forgot it.
